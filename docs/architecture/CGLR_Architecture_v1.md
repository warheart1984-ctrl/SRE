## Overview

![Constitutionally Governed Linguistic Reconstruction Architecture](assets/CGLR_Architecture_v1.png)

| Component | Role |
|-----------|------|
| **CIH** — Constitutional Kernel | Normative standard: invariants, gates, certification, scope enforcement |
| **Mythar** — Governed Proto-Language | Linguistic domain: lexicon, grammar, writing system, cosmology |
| **SRE** — Conformant Runtime | Executable reconstruction: FRA pipeline, alignment, validation, proto-form generation |
| **CEL** — Constitutional Evidence Ledger | Evidence plane: hash-chained lineage, certificates, governance traces |

---

## Data flows

**CIH → Mythar:** Governance, constraints, certification, scope enforcement

**SRE → Mythar:** Reconstruction, alignment, validation, proto-form generation

**SRE → CEL:** Evidence emission

**CEL → CIH:** Constitutional verification (feedback loop)

---

## Summary

> Mythar is the governed linguistic domain. CIH is the constitutional standard. SRE is the conformant runtime. CEL is the evidence plane. Together they form a reproducible, inspectable, academically valid reconstruction ecosystem.
