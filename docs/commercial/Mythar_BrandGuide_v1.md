# MYTHAR Brand Guide v1.0

**A constitutional linguistic brand system**  
**Owner:** Jon Halstead

---

## 1. Brand Essence

Mythar is the world’s first constitutionally governed proto-language, reconstructed through CIH, evidenced through CEL, and expressed through a living lexicon.

**Brand pillars:**

| Pillar | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Governed** | Every word has lineage |
| **Ancient-Modern** | Proto-language with modern governance |
| **Geometric** | Structured, axial, ritual |
| **Emergent** | Clusters grow organically |
| **Sacred-Technical** | Spiritual resonance, computational rigor |
