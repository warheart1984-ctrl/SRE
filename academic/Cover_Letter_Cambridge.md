## Framework vs proprietary boundary

**Mythar itself remains proprietary intellectual property.**  
This submission focuses on the **framework** (governance, evidence, methodology, runtime conformance), not licensed Mythar language content.

The cover letter draft referenced Mythar Writing System, Grammar, and Lexicon (Academic Edition). Those materials are **not** part of the Cambridge open submission unless separately licensed. Academic reviewers may evaluate:

- Constitutional governance of linguistic reconstruction
- Evidence-anchored proto-form derivation
- Reproducible reconstruction methodology
- Semantic axis cosmology (as methodology, not proprietary lexicon)
- Sound change modeling
- Ritual syntax structure (framework description only)
- CEL lineage transparency

Proprietary Mythar linguistic materials remain under `docs/mythar/publishable/` and require a separate license. See `docs/governance/CIH_Mythar_RelationshipCharter_v1.md`.
